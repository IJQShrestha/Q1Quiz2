Loving Vincent

Loving Vincent is a film about Vincent Van Gogh's death, and a some what detective ,Armand Roulin, who attempts to deliver Vincent Van Gogh's letter to his brother ,Theo Van Gogh, the letter was about a preview of his portait, "Portrait of a Peasant", he described what led him to make the painting.
In his journey he learns more and more about the artist, he learns things like how he was excluded, misunderstood, and his psychological problems.
